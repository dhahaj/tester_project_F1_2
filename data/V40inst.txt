Equipment Needed:
1) T-683 Test Base
2) V40/ADV Test Head
3) 104010-X PWA
<special equip>

Setup instructions for the V40:
1) Head unit is connected and secured to the test base.
2) PWA is clamped down on the head unit.
3) Test base is powered and blue LED is ON.
4) Test unit is connected to the computer, Serial COM LED is flasing.
4) Battery clip on PWA is connected to the test base.
5) All three slide switches should be OFF.
<special instructions>


Click "Program Device" in the Action menu or press "p"
  to begin programming.

After programming, click "Test Device" in the Action menu
  or press "t" to begin testing
